About



This application is a “Central Seller Hub”, which provides the seller a platform to add their products on a central hub. Using our application, seller can login into their account and add all products on their profile. This platform maps between seller and Ecommerce websites to register their product without adding it individually on each and every site. Apart from that The application has been completely automated using DevOps model with a single click deploy from development to deployment in production
